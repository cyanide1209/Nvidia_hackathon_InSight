MORE INFORMATION Your healthcare provider will diagnose pneumonia based on your medical history, a physical exam, and test results. Sometimes pneumonia is hard to diagnose because your symptoms may be the same as a cold or flu. You may not realize that your condition is more serious until it lasts longer than these other conditions.  Your provider will ask about your symptoms and when they began. They will also ask whether you have any risk factors for pneumonia.
